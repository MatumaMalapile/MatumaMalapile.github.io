My Name is Matuma Sophia Malapile

I am an Application Development student at CPUT with three years of coding experience. I work comfortably with Java, Python, and beginner-level JavaScript, and I hold a Higher Certificate in ICT (Application Development) as well as a Technical Software Development certificate.

I have completed projects such as an e-commerce desktop app, a student enrollment management system, and a UI redesign of the Apple Books App in Figma.

I am passionate about software development, user-centered design, and building professional web templates and websites.

I am passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems, create clean and intuitive digital experiences, and contribute to innovative projects. Whether it's building applications, designing professional web templates, or building websites for individuals and companies, I want everything I create to be reliable, modern, and crafted with purpose.
